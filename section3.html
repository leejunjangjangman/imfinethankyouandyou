About Cat Theory

01

Cat is liquid

The "cats are liquid" theory is a humorous observation about their flexibility. Cats can squeeze into almost any space, no matter how small, and seem to flow like water. This unique ability never fails to amaze and amuse cat lovers worldwide.

02

Cats defy gravity

Cats can leap from high places or walk along narrow ledges with perfect balance, as if gravity doesn't apply to them. T heir seemingly supernatural skills never fail to impress and entertain!

03

Batman in your house

Cats often disappear and reappear without a sound, observing their surroundings like vigilant protectors. Their love for the night and silent movements make them feel like tiny, furry superheroes in disguise!
